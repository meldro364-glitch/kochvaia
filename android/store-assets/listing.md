## Title
Kochvaia

## Short description (≤ 80 chars)
Track stars your kids earn and the rewards they're saving up for.

## Long description
Kochvaia is a calm, simple way for parents to celebrate good days and give
kids something to work toward.

**For parents**
• Add each kid with a name, emoji, and colour.
• Tap a day to give that kid a star. Backfill missed days when you remember.
• Build a rewards catalogue — "Pick the movie", "Dinner of your choice",
  whatever fits your family. Each reward has a star cost you choose.
• When stars run out, deduct from the oldest first.
• Get a friendly evening reminder if any kid hasn't received a star today —
  with one-tap action buttons right on the notification so you can give the
  star without opening the app.
• Invite a co-parent with a QR code. Both of you manage the same family.

**For kids**
• See how many stars they have right now.
• A 7-day strip shows when stars were earned — and a tap takes you to
  previous weeks.
• A family list lets siblings peek at each other's profiles. New stars
  arriving since the last visit play a little arrival animation.
• Rewards are listed with their star cost. Affordable ones light up. The
  ones still out of reach show how many more stars are needed.

**How it works**
• Parents sign in with their Google account.
• Kid devices pair with a QR code generated in the parent app — no kid
  accounts, no passwords for kids to remember.
• Data is stored on Cloudflare and is visible only to the family group.
• No ads. No tracking. No third-party data sharing.

Made for families who like turning the small wins of daily life into
something to celebrate together.

## App permissions (justifications, if asked)
• INTERNET — talk to the Kochvaia backend.
• CAMERA — scan the pairing QR code on the kid device (not used for photos).
• POST_NOTIFICATIONS — send the daily evening reminder when a kid hasn't
  received a star.
• VIBRATE — haptic confirmation when a parent taps a notification action.
